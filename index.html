Adoption Project

In my AP Research class, during the 2021-2022 school year, I created a website to collect data on the impact of international adoption on short term memory, which I then hosted on GoDaddy. This project recieved a 5/5 from CollegeBoard. (Disclaimer: I was not the one who conducted the research, I was only in charge of the data collection).
